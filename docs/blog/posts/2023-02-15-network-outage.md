# Rogers Communications Outage

## Summary
Rogers Communications, Canada's largest telecommunications provider, experienced a major core network failure affecting 12+ million customers across Ontario and Quebec.

## Timeline
- **February 15, 2023, 02:00 EST**: Core network router failure begins.
- **02:30 EST**: Widespread service degradation reported.
- **03:00 EST**: Complete outage affecting internet, cable TV, and wireless services.
- **06:00 EST**: Partial restoration begins.
- **12:00 EST**: Most services restored.
- **18:00 EST**: Full service recovery completed.

## Root Cause
A core network router failure in Rogers' primary data center caused cascading failures across their entire network infrastructure, affecting both wired and wireless services.

## Impact
- 12+ million customers affected across Ontario and Quebec.
- Complete loss of internet, cable TV, and wireless services.
- Emergency services disrupted in affected regions.
- Major business operations halted across Canada.

## Resolution
- Emergency failover to backup systems.
- Router replacement and network reconfiguration.
- Implementation of enhanced monitoring and redundancy.

## Lessons Learned
- Critical importance of network redundancy and failover systems.
- Need for comprehensive disaster recovery planning.
- Enhanced monitoring of core network infrastructure.
- Better communication with customers during major outages.
